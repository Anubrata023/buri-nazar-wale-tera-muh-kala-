# JanSaath 5-Minute Demo Script

## Setup (Before Stage)
- Tab 1: Citizen Portal (mobile view, iPhone 14)
- Tab 2: Admin Dashboard
- Tab 3: Public Transparency Dashboard
- Phone ready for IVR call (pre-dialed)
- Backup video ready on second device

---

## OPEN - The Hook (30 seconds)

**Person D says:**
"800 million Indians live in constituencies where their MP receives hundreds of complaints daily. There is no objective system to decide what gets fixed first. Decisions are made on gut feel and political pressure. The loudest voice wins. The most remote villages lose. JanSaath changes that. Let us show you how."

---

## SCENE 1 - The IVR (45 seconds)

**Person D says:**
"A farmer in Chinhat ward, Lucknow. No smartphone. No internet. 3,400 residents affected by a broken handpump. She calls our free number."

[Person A dials Twilio number on speaker]

"Voice greeting: Namaste. JanSaath mein aapka swagat hai. Beep ke baad boliye."

[Person D records: "Hamare mohalle mein handpump 3 hafte se band hai, pani nahi aa raha"]

"She hangs up. That's the entire interaction for the citizen."

[Person B switches to Citizen Portal - complaint appears in feed within 10 seconds]

"Gemini has transcribed, translated, and categorized it: Water Supply, Priority 8, 340 households affected."

---

## SCENE 2 - Mega-Issue Clustering (30 seconds)

**Person B navigates to Community Feed:**
"She was not alone. 47 other citizens reported the same handpump."

[Mega-Issue card shows "48 reports merged" with live upvote count]

"Our vector AI merged all 48 complaints into one Mega-Issue. The MP sees ONE problem, not 48 tickets. This single card carries the weight of 48 citizens' voices."

---

## SCENE 3 - Admin Dashboard (90 seconds)

**Person B switches to Admin tab:**
"This is what the MP sees. Not an inbox. An intelligence briefing."

[KPI row: Total Issues: 247, Resolved this month: 89, Avg resolution: 6.2 days]

[Lucknow heatmap - Chinhat ward glowing red]

"Every issue has a priority score calculated by a transparent mathematical formula - not an AI guess."

[Click Handpump Mega-Issue - Insight Panel slides in]

**Insight Panel shows:**
- 🛰️ Satellite thumbnail: Chinhat ward (Earth Engine - free tier)
- 👥 Affected: 3,400 residents within 500m (Census 2021 data)
- 🚰 Nearest water source: 1.4km away (OpenStreetMap)
- 💰 Cost estimate: Rs 45,000 (scikit-learn model trained on MGNREGS data)
- 📜 Scheme match: Jal Jeevan Mission - Eligible

"All this - from Census, OpenStreetMap, budget, and government scheme data - assembled in 1.5 seconds."

[Click Draft Proposal - Google Doc opens in 3 seconds]

"What used to take a junior official 3 days now takes 3 seconds."

---

## SCENE 4 - Closing the Loop (30 seconds)

**Person B drags Kanban card:**
"The MP approves. One drag."

[Drag card from "New" to "Funds Allocated"]

[Phone receives SMS: "Aapki shikayat manzoor hui. Kaam shuru hoga 15 July tak."]

[Switch to Public Transparency tab]

"And this resolution is now public. Any journalist. Any citizen. Real time."

---

## SCENE 5 - The Prediction (30 seconds)

**Person B shows Forecast Heatmap:**
"One more thing. JanSaath doesn't just respond to problems. It predicts them."

[Forecast heatmap - Ward 12 highlighted orange]

"Our Prophet model predicts Ward 12 will have 60% more water complaints in August - before the monsoon arrives."

"The MP pre-allocates budget TODAY. This is proactive governance."

---

## CLOSE - The Pitch (15 seconds)

**Person D delivers the close:**
"From a voice call on a Rs 500 phone to a funded government project with satellite-verified evidence and public accountability - in under 5 minutes. All built on free infrastructure. JanSaath. For 1.4 billion citizens. Built on Google AI."
